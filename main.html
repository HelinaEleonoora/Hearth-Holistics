My Services

  • Full body massage sessions (including side body and belly) with a holistic approach
  • Traditional Chinese Cupping and Quasha Therapy

Whether you long for pure relaxation with a soft touch, or require a dynamic deep tissue massage centered around tense areas, my sessions are always shaped for your specific desires.

The transformative bodywork practice I offer is a fusion of Alchemy of Touch with some elements of traditional Chinese medicine and massage.

According to traditional Chinese medicine, the body has its own pattern of channels through which life-force energy flows. This energy is called Qi, and the channels are the Meridians. Blockages and imbalances in these Meridians stop the natural flow of Qi. They can cause discomfort, or even disease. With the help of massage, cupping or guasha these imbalances and stagnations can be gently addressed. Bodywork has been a part of traditional Chinese medicine for thousands of years. The earliest record on massage is in the inscriptions on bones and tortoise shells of the Shang Dynasty (16th -11th century BC).

Alchemy of Touch is a dynamic and poetic form of transformational bodywork, developed by Tapesh Paradiso and Anouk Devi. This form of bodywork unites Eastern and Western modalities and different techniques are blent together. In my sessions I focus on the release of tension and the opening of energetic channels. Unique to Alchemy of Touch is the so called ‘Fluid Touch’ method. This technique uses wavelike movements, which allow energy to vibrate through the body improving fluidity and oxygen flow. About Me

My name is Helinä and I began my bodywork journey in 2017. I have been a massage therapist in clinics where I gave (Chinese) massage therapy among other treatments. In these times I got a ‘feeling’ for treating blockages, trigger points and the overall tension people might struggle with. I also learned some basic knowledge about the meridians, acupoints and anatomy.

After a while I aimed to give a more individual and deepening touch to my sessions. I got more interested in the body-mind-spirit connection and developed a holistic approach. In order to deepen my practice I found a training that resonated with me, called Alchemy of Touch, and I am certified with both level 1 and 2.

I enjoy giving every client my full attention and the time needed for turning inward and listen to what your body needs. To transform tension into a meditative and flowing expierence.
